Paper folding day!

2020-02-14 2 min read # folding paper # rose

The 2020 valentine's day is coming soon~
I will folding a rose for my husband, cos I can't go outside to buy one.
now let's begin!

1 step:
now I prepare 4 papers, 3 is red and 1 is green, the size is 15cm, 11cm, 9cm, 6cm, and the 13cm green one.

2 step:
Start following step to folding the 15cm red one.
-Folding the paper quarter to quarter, made a cross in middle.

-Folding the paper from four quarters to middle.

-Fold up again.

-And again.

-Folding again from back side.

-Then start fold each quarter from the 1/2 middle line.

-Turn back the paper, the gathering the four quarters to middle.

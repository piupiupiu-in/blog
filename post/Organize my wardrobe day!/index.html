Organize my wardrobe day!

2020-02-09 1 min read # clothing

Organize my wardrobe always in my mind,
but never done...
In the such a long Chinese 2020 new year holiday😷, I decided to clean the room and clear my personal organize theory.

✅1 step:
Separate the clothings to 6 big areas and 22 small styles.
You can separate your clothings by your personal styles.

✅2 step:
Making my clothing arrange rolls.
I design two sizes for different styles.
small size: top/blouse/pants/skirt/thin sweater.
big size: hoodies/thick sweater.

✅3 step:

Rolling one piece of clothing into the arrange roll.
white one is small size, yellow one is big size.

✅4 step:

Putting different color rolls in different area.
Separate the wardrobe bulkhead to 3 parts.
(pants+skirt, top+blouse, hoodies+sweater).

✅5 step:
Putting all the rolls on it area, and hanging easy wrinkle clothing on hangers.

Cup case of my family~

2020-03-29 1 min read

This saturday, my husband buy two cups on line, but we found them can't hold after hot and clod water fill in, so I design two cup case for them~

First step:
Drawing the pattern of case shape follow the cup surface, and cut them out.
Then:
Pin the orange nonwoven and canvas fabric together.

The nonwoven fabric will be inside layer and the canvas fabric will be surface.
After that:
Using triangle stitch sew the two piece together.


The last step:
Sewing their two edge together, then finish~
